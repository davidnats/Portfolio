Mobility Work is the first community-based, next-gen maintenance management platform, offering both a CMMS (computerized maintenance management system) solution available in SaaS and a social network dedicated to maintenance professionals and their suppliers.
With more than 35,000 users spread across the world, the Mobility Work CMMS community benefits from the experience of over 7 million hours of maintenance, and exchanges information on the nearly 1 million equipment already created.
