The On/Go app is here to give you peace of mind through the testing process for COVID-19. Instead of reading through a sheet of instructions bigger than a world map, we give you simple, clear, visual instructions at the touch of your fingertips. Follow the instructions as you take your On/Go test. The app walks you through all the steps, from prep, to sample collection, to reading the results. The best part is you can store, view, and share your test results (only with those you want to share with). This allows you to easily show your results as you get back out there— from events, to work, to parties, and more. This app can be used with the On/Go and CareStart OTC Self-Tests.
